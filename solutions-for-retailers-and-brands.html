Brand & Retailers

It’s a fact, sometimes you need more warehouse space than you have. Whether you are experiencing seasonal inventory peaks, want to expand into new markets, release new products, or find that your demand planning and supply chain are out of sync; trying to find quality space in a hurry is tough, expensive, and disruptive to your business activities. But with Warehousing On-Demand by FLUENCE overflow solutions, the pain that comes with sourcing additional warehousing space and services disappears.

FLUENCE connects you to professionally operated warehouses with the overflow space you need for palette storage, handling, and deconsolidation services for only as long as you need it. Our powerful software platform gives you operational control and transparency into your shipments; from at-a-glance reporting to detailed granular analysis, you know exactly where your goods are in real time. The FLUENCE software also consolidates all of your billing with one set of terms on one platform, so working with multiple warehouses across South Africa is effortless.

It’s the perfect complement to your existing distribution infrastructure. The best part? You can be up and running fast because no IT integration is required. Ready to spend more time on what matters most and less time scrambling for space? Then you are ready for FLUENCE.

E-Commerce

Thanks to technology, consumers expect their orders to be delivered fast with low-cost or even free shipping, which leaves e-commerce operations like yours looking for ways to reduce shipping times and costs. But opening more fulfilment centres is expensive, requires long lead times, and comes with long term commitments. Wouldn’t it be great if you could offer fast, low cost shipping, without the time and capital commitments of a new fulfilment centre? Well, with FLUENCE On-Demand fulfilment you can.

FLUENCE connects you to warehouses that offer fulfilment services so you can pop up new fulfilment centres anywhere, anytime. Here’s how, through the FUENCE Software find warehouses in the location you need and can figure your fulfilment operations to get full visibility to all your shipments and inventory, consolidated billing, and one set of terms across every fulfilment partner in the FLUENCE Marketplace. Just like that you are adding fulfilment centres to your network, and providing fast low-cost shipping to scale your business, better manage peak seasons, and strategically deliver on e-commerce product promotions.

Solutions For Warehouse Operators

Lower Supply Chain Costs

The FLUENCE marketplace enables you to provide warehouse storage and services to other businesses on your own terms, generating new revenue and lowering overall supply chain costs.

Bulk Storage Solutions - FLUENCE allows you to offer space and services on-demand via our marketplace. It’s easy to list your space and you maintain total control over the projects you accept. All workflow and terms are already formalized into the system.

Fulfillment Solutions - Increase your revenue-generating capabilities by offering additional pick and pack services and turning your warehouse into a fulfillment center. The FLUENCE marketplace connects you to shippers that need to expand their fulfillment operations.

Warehouse Operators

Seasonal slow periods, uncertainty in growth forecast, and changes to your supply chain strategy affect your warehouse utilisation and can be a drag on your P&O. At the same time shippers across the industry are searching for flexible warehousing and fulfilment solutions to manage inventory overflow and e-commerce fulfilment needs. What if you could provide that flexibility through on-demand warehousing solution? Thereby filling your excess warehouse capacity and more fully utilising your labour all year long; and most importantly, creating a new source of revenue for your business. With FLUENCE you can.

FLUENCE connects shippers across South Africa to warehouse operators like you for on-demand warehouse services, and our technology makes it easy for you. Here’s how it works: first, sign up for FLUENCE Online. It’s free to join, and upon approval your listing will be up and running in minutes. Next, you will start receiving projects that match your capabilities. You are in complete control, you set your pricing and you choose which products you want to accept. Once you approve a project, shipment starts arriving at your warehouse, and it’s all managed through our free cloud-based Warehouse Management Software.

You get paid for your space and services, generating new and high margin revenue. It’s as simple as that.
